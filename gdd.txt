Crazy Tanks
Game Design Document
---------------------------

- Open world head to head tank combat.
- Terrain will be used for tactical advantage
- Main focus: flow and feel


Rules
- Move anywhere in the terrain which is surrounded by mountains
- Both players start with finite ammo and HP
- Each direct hit takes away HP

Requirements
- SFX: gunfire, explosions, barrel moving, turret mobing, engine sound
- Static meshes: simple tank with tracks, body turret and barrel
- Textures for visuals
- Background music
